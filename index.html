ALERT DEVICE

This a project started by NISH Trivandrum for helping the speech and hearing impaired people. This device is to be used in the library.

  • WEEK 1:
    • Components required to design the circuit were identified and listed.
    • A block diagram of the circuit was drawn.
    • Some circuits were done for study purpose
    • We also fixed a timeline according to which the project was to be completed.

      • A basic stage of the circuit was made and temporarily fixed onto a breadboard.

  • WEEK 2:
    • The project was implemented using NodeMCU
    • Using MIT appinventor, an app was created.
    • Fabricated the power supply and regulator sytem.
    • Measurements were taken for making CAD design.
    • Different trials were carried out for CAD design, and finally fixed a model based on aesthetics and ergonomics.
